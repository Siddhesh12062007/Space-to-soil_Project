Controlled Environment Agriculture

Our research focuses on developing plant growth systems that can operate efficiently in the challenging conditions of space, including microgravity, limited resources, and confined spaces.

We're testing various crops for their suitability in space environments, with focus on nutritional density, growth efficiency, and resilience to space conditions.

Current research includes optimizing LED light recipes, developing automated nutrient delivery systems, and creating closed-loop water recycling for maximum efficiency.
